## Detailed Description

### Stop losing money on COD returns

Indian D2C brands lose crores every year to COD returns. 39% of COD orders come back — costing you shipping both ways plus lost revenue.

**Taskly Returns** automates your entire return process — from customer request to refund — saving you 10+ hours per week.

### What you get:

**Self-Service Return Portal**
Give your customers a branded return page. They submit requests in 3 clicks — no WhatsApp messages, no phone calls, no manual work.

**Works With Any Payment Gateway**
Razorpay, Paytm, PhonePe, Cashfree — doesn't matter. Refunds process automatically through Shopify's built-in system.

**Works With Any Courier**
Shiprocket, Delhivery, BlueDart, Ecom Express — connect your preferred shipping aggregator for automated pickup scheduling.

**Smart Dashboard**
See all returns in one place. Filter by status, track refund amounts, identify top return reasons, and make data-driven decisions.

**WhatsApp Notifications**
Auto-notify customers at every step — request received, approved, pickup scheduled, refund processed. No manual follow-ups.

**Exchange Instead of Refund**
Offer exchanges to retain revenue. Customer picks a new size or color — you keep the sale.

**Store Credit Option**
Offer store credit as a refund method. Customers come back to shop again.

### How it works:
1. Install the app (free, 2 minutes)
2. Connect your Shopify store
3. Share your return portal link with customers
4. Manage returns from your dashboard
